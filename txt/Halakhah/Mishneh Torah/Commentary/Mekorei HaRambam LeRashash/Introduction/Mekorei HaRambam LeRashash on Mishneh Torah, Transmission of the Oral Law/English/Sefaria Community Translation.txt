Halakhah 1

"The Torah" refers to the written Torah, and "the mitzvah" refers to its interpretation. In Berakhot (5), "The Torah" refers to the Mikra (Scripture), and "the mitzvah" refers to the Mishnah, etc.

Halakhah 2

"And He gave a Sefer (Torah scroll) to each tribe." This is stated in the Midrash Rabah, at the end of Parashat Vayelech.

Halakhah 19

"And among the sages who received from Rava are Rav Ashi and Ravina. This is surprising, for in Kiddushin (72b) it is written that when Rava passed away, Rav Ashi was born. And see in the *Seder HaDorot*, in the section on the Tanaim and Amoraim, about Rav Ashi."
