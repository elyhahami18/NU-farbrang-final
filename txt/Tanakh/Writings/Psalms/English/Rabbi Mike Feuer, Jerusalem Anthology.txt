Chapter 48

A song, a psalm of the sons of Korah. 
The Lord is great and very much praised, in the city of our God, the Mount of His Sanctuary. 
The fairest of branches, the joy of the entire earth- Mount Zion, by the north side, the city of a great king. 
God is in its palaces; He is known as a stronghold.

Chapter 60










Moab is my washbasin; on Edom I will throw my lock; Philistia, join me. 
Who will bring me to a fortified city?-He Who led me to Edom? 
Is it not You, O God, Who has forsaken us, and [Who] does not go forth, O God, with our hosts? 
Give us aid against the adversary, but the salvation of man is futile. 
Through God we shall gather might, and He will trample our adversaries. (yevus tsareinu)
