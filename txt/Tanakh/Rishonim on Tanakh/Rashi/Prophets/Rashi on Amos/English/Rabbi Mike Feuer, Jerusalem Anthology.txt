Verse 8

swore by Himself – consciously and with intention: 
I abhor – This means abhor (even though it is written with an aleph and not an ayin.) This is how Yonatan translated it into Aramaic ‘I despise the great Holy Temple of Yaakov.’ He calls it this because the Temple is called the pride of their strength, as it says “Behold I am profaning My sanctuary, the pride of your strength…” (Ezekiel 24:21): 
the city and the fullness thereof  - Jerusalem and its inhabitants:
