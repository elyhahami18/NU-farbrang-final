"And in the kingship of Achashverosh"- The king after Cyrus, the Achashverosh who took Esther [for a wife].
"Wrote an accusation"-[a decree] not to build the Holy Temple.
"On the settlers of Judah and Jerusalem"- They changed [what they had previously said] to instigate/cause trouble, that they would not build the Holy Temple
