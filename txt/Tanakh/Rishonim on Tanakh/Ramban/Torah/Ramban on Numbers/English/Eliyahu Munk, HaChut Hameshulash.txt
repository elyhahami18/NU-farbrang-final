Verse 4

ויפל על פניו, only Moses, not Aaron; Aaron preserved his dignity by not displaying any reaction to the rebellion at all, seeing that he was the one who had aroused this jealousy. Although his silence and lack of defense of himself could be interpreted as an admission that the insurgents were right, he obeyed Moses’ instructions as one obeys the decree of one’s king.
