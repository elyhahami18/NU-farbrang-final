And from this, it appears to me that it is permitted to give an inoculation against smallpox, even though one in a thousand will die as a result of the inoculation, nevertheless, if the smallpox is left untreated, the danger to life is much more imminent. Therefore one is permitted to put themselves in a distant (less likely) danger in order to save themselves from a closer (more likely) danger...
