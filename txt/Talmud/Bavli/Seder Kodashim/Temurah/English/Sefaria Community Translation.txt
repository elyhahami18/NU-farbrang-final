Immediately, Joshua's strength withered and he forgot 3000 of Moshe's teachings and practical laws, and parts of the law, and he had 7000 doubts about other legal matters.  All of the children of Israel wanted to kill him.  He asked G-d what to do.  G-d responded, "it is impossible (to be equal to Moshe), go on, and rather occupy the people with warfare.
